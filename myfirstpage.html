Introduction to Western Art History of Paintings

The art of Europe, or Western art, encompasses the history of visual art in Europe. European art is arranged into a number of stylistic periods, which, historically, overlap each other as different styles flourished in different areas. Broadly the periods are, Renaissance, Modernism, Romanticism, & Modern Art.
